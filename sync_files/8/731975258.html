The Argumentative Indian

he Argumentative Indian is a book written by Nobel Prize winning Indian economist Amartya Sen. It is a collection of essays that discuss India's history and identity, focusing on the traditions of public debate and intellectual pluralism. The book demonstrates the importance of public debate in Indian traditions generally.
